Challenging the Tier List

The scour didn't just fill gaps — it changed the ranking. Here's where I'd revise the portfolio, and the calendar traps to plan around.

Re-tier recommendations

Fresh-eyes moves based on what the research surfaced.

Add

Atlanta — glaringly absent, yet ~2h away with a top-5 world aquarium, Zoo Atlanta, Fernbank & Stone Mountain. Make it a formal hub and Tier-1 mini-destination; it doubles as your Florida-route launch point.

Add

Charleston — hits walkability, food, beaches, history & a sea-turtle-hospital aquarium better than several Tier-2 picks. Belongs in Tier 1–2.

Add

Knoxville — your gateway to the entire KY/OH corridor, and a real destination itself (Zoo Knoxville, Ijams, the Smokies 45 min on). Promote to a hub.

Elevate

Huntsville (from Tier 3) — Space & Rocket Center + Cook Museum is the best science-and-engineering day in range, tailor-made for your 4th grader.

Elevate

Columbus (from Tier 3) — COSI + Jack Hanna's zoo + The Wilds safari out-punch Indianapolis for your themes.

Reconsider

Philadelphia — at 9.5h it competes with closer, richer DC. Treat it as a fly, not drive trip, or pair it onto a DC itinerary rather than its own quarterly.

Note

St. Louis earns its spot (City Museum + free world-class zoo), but its 9.5h drive only works via the Chattanooga→Nashville chain — name that dependency in its playbook.

Verify before you book

Timing flags the agents surfaced — check official sites near your dates.

Nov 15–Mar 31

Crystal River manatee swims only run in season. Off-season, Homosassa Springs' underwater "Fish Bowl" has manatees year-round.

Oct 26 '26–
Mar 24 '27

Universal's Volcano Bay closed for refurb — pivot to Aquatica if you're in Orlando that window.

Thru mid-Jul '26

Nashville's Parthenon closed for HVAC work — verify reopening; the free park around it stays open.

2026 season

Savannah Bananas use a ticket lottery and travel much of the year — confirm home dates and enter early.

From Apr 29 '26

Rainbow Springs headspring now requires reservations to enter.

Location

Mote SEA is the new Sarasota/UTC aquarium — the old City Island Mote is closed. Go to UTC.

Age minimums

Discovery Cove dolphin swim & Devil's Den snorkel are 6+, sidelining your 5-year-old from that one activity (easy on-site alternatives exist).

Seasonal

Most outdoor water parks & spring tubing run ~late-May–early-Aug only. Confirm 2026 dates.
